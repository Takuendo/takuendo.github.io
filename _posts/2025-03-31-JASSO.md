I am pleased to share that I have been granted a half-exemption of my JASSO (Japan Student Services Organization) scholarship repayment (¥88,000 × 24 months).

This exemption is awarded to graduates who have demonstrated outstanding academic or research performance during their scholarship period. It recognizes the efforts and achievements made during my time as a master's student at the University of Tokyo.

I am grateful to my supervisors, mentors, and collaborators who supported me throughout my studies in Japan.
